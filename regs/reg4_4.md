# Fraternity Regulation 4.4 (Original)

## CHAPTER AFFAIRS / Chapter Standards

Effective August 1, 2025

<strong>
4.4.1.
</strong>
The purpose of TRIANGLE, as stated in the Article 2 of the National Constitution, shall be "to maintain a fraternity of engineers, architects, and scientists. It shall carry out its purpose by establishing chapters that develop balanced men who cultivate high moral character, foster lifelong friendships, and live with integrity.""

<strong>
4.4.2.
</strong>
Every chapter of TRIANGLE has a responsibility to uphold this purpose and develop programming, practices, and traditions that serve these goals.

<strong>
4.4.3.
</strong>
The Triangle Fraternity Chapter Standards are intended to identify chapter expectations that align with the fraternity's purpose and serve as a tool to monitor chapter success. Performance related to these standards will be utilized in determining chapter status, support, awards, and if necessary, disciplinary action.

<strong>
4.4.4.
</strong>
OBSERVE THE PRECEPTS OF THE FRATERNITY AS SET FORTH IN THE RITUAL
<ol type="a">
<li>A review of the Ritual shall be conducted at least once per term</li>
</ol>

<strong>
4.4.5.
</strong>
ACCEPT CHEERFULLY MY FULL SHARE OF ANY TASK, HOWEVER MENIAL, INVOLVED IN MAINTAINING A CHAPTER HOME
<ol type="a">
<li>A chapter has a three-year rolling average membership size of at least thirty (30) active members</li>
<li>A chapter has at least five (5) officers, including President, Vice President, Secretary, Risk Manager/Prevention Chair, and Treasurer. Alternative equivalent titles may be allowed. The President and Treasurer shall be elected for a one-year term unless otherwise approved by the National Headquarters</li>
<li>A chapter has chapter coaches qualified via the National program and a faculty advisor</li>
<li>A chapter hosts weekly chapter meetings</li>
<li>A chapter maintains an accurate record of active members and candidates via the Triangle Portal</li>
<li>A chapter notifies the National Headquarters of new candidates and initiates within the time periods specified in the National Bylaws</li>
</ol>

<strong>
4.4.6.
</strong>
PRESERVE AND PROMOTE THE CHOSEN IDEALS OF MY FRATERNITY
<ol type="a">
<li>A chapter sends representatives to National Convention in all years that the event is held</li>
<li>A chapter sends representatives to Leadership and Training Weekend and Herbert Scobie Leadership School in all years that the events are held when adequate funds are available to offset program costs</li>
</ol>

<strong>
4.4.7.
</strong>
PAY ALL PERSONAL BILLS PROMPTLY, AND ALWAYS LIVE WITHIN MY MEANS
<ol type="a">
<li>A chapter has filed a current IRS 990 form</li>
<li>A chapter pays all national, insurance program, and local bills in a timely manner (no bills past
90 days due)</li>
<li>A chapter submits an annual budget via the Triangle Portal</li>
</ol>

<strong>
4.4.8.
</strong>
HELP CREATE IN MY CHAPTER HOME AN ENVIRONMENT IN WHICH ENDURING FRIENDSHIPS MAY BE FORMED
<ol type="a">
<li>A chapter hosts at least one brotherhood and one alumni event each academic year</li>
<li>A chapter hosts an appropriate celebration of Triangle Founder's Day annually</li>
<li>A chapter with shared housing, whether it is owned, rented, or otherwise designated, shall have chapter bylaws and policies that regulate the requirements for individual members to live in the facility. The chapter shall have a policy for ensuring that its facility is filled whenever conditions permit and establishing a method for who may live out of the facility.</li>
</ol>

<strong>
4.4.9.
</strong>
MAINTAIN A CREDITABLE SCHOLASTIC RECORD
<ol type="a">
<li>A chapter only initiates candidates that meet the minimum qualification for membership (minimum GPA of 2.5 at the time of initiation)</li>
<li>A chapter maintains an average chapter GPA of 2.7 or above and an average candidate GPA of 2.7 or above</li>
</ol>

<strong>
4.4.10.
</strong>
PROMOTE THE WELFARE OF MY PROFESSION
<ol type="a">
<li>At least fifty percent (50%) of the active members of a chapter are members of a professional organization</li>
</ol>

<strong>
4.4.11.
</strong>
MAINTAIN MY SELF-RESPECT BY PROPER CONDUCT AT ALL TIMES
<ol type="a">
<li>All active members and candidates of a chapter have completed all risk prevention education required by TRIANGLE and by their university</li>
</ol>

<strong>
4.4.12.
</strong>
UPHOLD FAITHFULLY THE TRADITIONS AND PROGRAM OF MY ALMA MATER
<ol type="a">
<li>A chapter’s community service level meets or exceeds the minimum mandated by their campus’s governing council, and is at least ten (10) hours per member per year</li>
</ol>
